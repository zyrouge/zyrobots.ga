Music Commands

Play/Join/P [name/url] - Plays a Song
Search/Se [name] - Searches for the Song and Gives you 10 Choices
Queue/Q/SongList/MusicList/List - Shows the Music Queue
Pause/Pa - Pauses the Song
Mute/m - Mutes the Bot in VC
Unmute/unm - Unmutes the Bot in VC
Resume/R - Resumes the Song
NowPlaying/NP - Shows the Currently Playing Song
Skip/S - Skips the Song/Voting Included
Volume/Vol/V [1-100] - Sets the Volume
